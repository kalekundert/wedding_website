We’ve been fortunate to benefit in both love and in life.
We also recognize that gift-giving causes people strife.

If choosing a gift is not your cup of tea,
You might find ideas in our Amazon Registry.

In lieu of traditional gifts, and we mean this with sincerity,
We’d also be honored if you donated to a charity:
National Multiple Sclerosis Society
Natural Resource Defense Council
American Civil Liberties Union
Charity: Water

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift of all.
We can’t wait to see you! We’re sure it will be a ball!
