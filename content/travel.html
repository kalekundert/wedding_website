Locations

Most of the events will be centered around historic downtown Wilmington (i.e. Front and Market). The ceremony itself will be held at 111 N. 15th St.

Flying

The nearest airport is Wilmington International Airport (ILM) which is located just 7 miles from downtown Wilmington.

Other nearby airports include:

  • Fayetteville, NC (FAY)
  • Myrtle Beach, SC (MYR)
  • Richlands, NC (OAJ)
  • Raleigh, NC (RDU)
  • Charlotte, NC (CLT)

Driving

From the North/South: Take I-95. Connect to I-74E towards Wilmington NC.
From the West: Take I-40E or I-74E to Wilmington, NC.
