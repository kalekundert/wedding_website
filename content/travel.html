Flying

The nearest airport is Wilmington International Airport (ILM) which is located just 7 miles from downtown Wilmington.

Other nearby domestic airports include:

  • Fayetteville, NC (FAY)
  • Myrtle Beach, SC (MYR)
  • Richlands, NC (OAJ)

If you are flying internationally, we recommend connecting through these major cities:

  • Charleston, SC (CHS)
  • Raleigh, NC (RDU)

Driving

From the North/South: Take I-95 and connect to I-74E towards Wilmington NC. From the West: Take I-40E or I-74E to Wilmington, NC.
